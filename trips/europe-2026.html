Vintgar + Savica + Radovljica 🎂
Jun 29 · Bled base · Veena's Birthday! 🎉
🇸🇮
▾
🎂 Happy Birthday Veena! — celebrated in one of the most beautiful places on Earth, Lake Bled, Slovenia.
📍 Day 10 Map
  • 9:00am — Vintgar Gorge (pre-booked timed entry mandatory) · 1.6-mile boardwalk over blue-green water (~1.5hrs)
  • Drive 30 min → Savica Waterfall (78m cascade, 558 steps, ~1.5hrs)

  • Bled cream cake at Park Café — the iconic local dessert, perfect as a birthday treat!
  • Pletna boat to Bled Island — traditional hand-rowed wooden boat, ring the wishing bell
  • Special birthday dinner lakeside — try Vila Bled or Restavracija Belvedere for the occasion
