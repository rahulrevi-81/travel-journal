1–2
Iceland — Golden Circle & South Coast
Jun 20–21 · Selfoss base
🇮🇸
▾
📍 Day 1 Map — Golden Circle 📍 Day 2 Map — South Coast

Day 1 — Golden Circle

  • Pick up Iceland car at KEF ~10am
  • Þingvellir NP — UNESCO · walk between the tectonic plates
  • Geysir — Strokkur erupts every 5–10 min
  • Gullfoss waterfall
  • Back to Selfoss for overnight (~45 min drive)

Day 2 — South Coast

  • Seljalandsfoss — walk behind the falls · bring waterproofs!
  • Skógafoss waterfall
  • Reynisfjara black sand beach
  • Back to Selfoss for overnight (~1hr drive)
⏰ Set alarm 5:15am Jun 22 — drive ~45 min to KEF ✓ SelfossCenter Flat · Booking.com

Grindelwald First Cliff Walk

🇨🇭
▾
📍 Day 4 Map
  • Leave 9:00am → drive ~1hr to Grindelwald · arrive ~10:00am
  • Firstbahn gondola up → First (2,166m) · Cliff Walk (~30 min) · Bachalpsee lake hike (~2.5 miles, Eiger reflections, ~1.5hrs)

  • Gondola Bort → Grindelwald (8 min) · back at car ~2:30pm
  • Rösti lunch in Grindelwald · drive ~1.5hrs to Schneizlreuth · arrive ~5:30pm

Liechtensteinklamm Gorge
Jun 25 · Bad Goisern
🇦🇹
▾
📍 Day 6 Map
  • Leave 7:30am → 1.5hrs to Liechtensteinklamm Gorge (arrive 9am)
  • Deepest gorge in Alps — Helix staircase, 400 stairs, thundering waterfall (~2.5hrs, ~€15/adult)
  • Drive 1hr back to Bad Goisern ~1pm
  • Afternoon: 10 min to Hallstatt for evening lakeside walk
✓ HOMEBOUND Apartments · Expedia #72072198804917
7
Hallstatt Full Day
Jun 26 · Bad Goisern base
🇦🇹
▾
📍 Day 7 Map
  • Arrive Hallstatt 8:45am (before crowds)
  • Echern Valley → Waldbachstrub Waterfall (~3 miles, ~2.5hrs)
  • Hallstatt Skywalk (Welterbeblick) — free viewpoint, best panoramic view of lake and village
  • Lunch lakeside · afternoon: lake boat or relax
  • Dinner lakeside — try local char (Saibling)

🇦🇹🇸🇮
▾
📍 Day 8 Map
  • Checkout 10am
  • Gosausee — optional (2.85-mile flat loop) · only if energy allows
  • Drive south → Grossglockner High Alpine Road (2,504m, glacier views, toll €45/car · pack warm layers)
  • Drive ~2.5hrs east → arrive Lake Bled ~9pm (long daylight in late June)

9
Lake Bled
Jun 28 · Bled base
🇸🇮
▾
📍 Day 9 Map
  • 7:30am — Mala Osojnica viewpoint (20-min hike, best view of Bled Island)
  • Bled Castle — clifftop fortress, panoramic views (~1.5hrs, €13/adult)
  • Bled cream cake at Park Café — a local institution
  • Mostnica Gorge — forest trail, Devil's Bridge, ~2 miles
  • Late afternoon: drive 30 min to Vogel Cable Car at Bohinj — panoramic Julian Alps views
10
Vintgar + Savica + Radovljica
Jun 29 · Bled base
🇸🇮
▾
📍 Day 10 Map
  • 9:00am — Vintgar Gorge (pre-booked timed entry mandatory) · 1.6-mile boardwalk over blue-green water (~1.5hrs)
  • Drive 30 min → Savica Waterfall (78m cascade, 558 steps, ~1.5hrs)
  • Radovljica old town — medieval town, gingerbread workshop, beekeeping museum (~1hr)
  • Back to Bled for overnight
⏰ Book Vintgar timed entry by May 1 — vintgar.si
