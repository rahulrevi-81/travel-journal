Key Reminders

⛰️
Altitude sickness is real — Cusco is at 11,152 ft. Drink lots of water, take it slow Day 2, consider altitude medication
🌿
Coca tea — widely available and helps with altitude. Try it as soon as you arrive
🎟️
Machu Picchu tickets are timed and non-transferable — don't miss entry windows (6am Circuit 1B, 3pm Circuit 2B)
🌡️
Pack layers — mornings are cold at altitude, afternoons can be warm, and it can rain any time
💧
Drink bottled water only — tap water in Peru is not safe to drink
💵
Carry Soles cash — many smaller restaurants and markets don't accept cards. ATMs available in Cusco
🌧️
December = rainy season — bring ponchos and waterproof bags for everything
🚂
Inca Rail is booked — print or download tickets before the trip, cell signal is patchy
