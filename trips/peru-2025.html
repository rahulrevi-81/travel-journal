Key Reminders

🏔️
Cusco sits at 11,150 ft — plan a rest day on arrival to acclimatize properly
💧
Drink plenty of water and avoid alcohol the first 2 days at altitude
🎫
Machu Picchu tickets are timed-slot entry — don't miss your window
🥊
Nights in Cusco and Ollantaytambo are cold even in December — pack warm layers
💵
Carry Peruvian Soles for local markets and tips — cards not always accepted
🌧️
December is start of rainy season in Peru — pack a poncho or waterproof jacket
