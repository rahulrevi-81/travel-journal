Key Reminders

🏱️
Alaska in June has very long daylight — bring eye masks for sleeping (up to 19hrs of daylight)
🌛️
Weather changes fast — always pack layers and a waterproof jacket even on sunny days
🐻
Wildlife is everywhere — keep distance from bears, moose and other wildlife at all times
⛽
Denali Highway is a gravel road — drive slowly, check tire pressure before and after
📷
Day 8's ~11hr drive is long but spectacular — start early and build in photo stops
💧
Carry bear spray on all hikes and know how to use it
