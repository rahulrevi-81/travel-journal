Important Reminders
✅
112 works everywhere on this trip Iceland, Switzerland, Germany, Austria, Italy — one number for police, fire, and ambulance. Free from any phone including no-SIM phones.
💳
Cards work across Europe Visa/Mastercard accepted widely. Keep some cash for mountain huts (rifugios, hüttes) — cash only. ATMs widely available in all towns.
⛰️
Mountain safety If someone is injured on a hike: call 112, stay calm, give your GPS coordinates. Most phones show coordinates in compass app. Mountain rescue is free in Austria and Italy.
